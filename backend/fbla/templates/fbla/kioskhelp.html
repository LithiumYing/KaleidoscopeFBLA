Help Page for Kiosk

Welcome to the help page. Please select a question from the left-hand bar to get help with whatever you are trying to do.

How do I select an event?

Step 1

Click on the dropdown. This will show all the available events.

Step 2

Find the event, and click on it to select the event.

/>

Step 3

Click on the continue button. The sign in/out screen will appear, and students can then begin signing in/out.

How do I create an event?

Step 1

Head over to the admin page.

Step 2

Click "Events".

Step 3

Click "Add Event" in the top right corner.

Step 3

Enter all the info for the event you want to add, then click "Save".

Why isn't my event showing up?

The kiosk software will only show events that are available to sign in. If an event isn't showing up, check on the admin panel.
