How do I add students?

There are two ways to add students. Individually, and through bulk importing.

Individual

Step 1

Go to the admin home page. Click "Students".

Step 2

Click "Add Student" in the top right corner.

Step 3

Enter all the info for the student you want to add, then click "Save".


Bulk (CSV Import)

Step 1

Go to the admin home page. Click "Students".

Step 2

Click "Import Data from CSV" in top right corner

Step 3

Upload your CSV file of student data, then click "Import". The CSV file should use the format shown in the below image.

How do I generate a report?

Step 1

Go to the admin home page. Click "Reports".

Step 2

Click "Add Report" in the top right corner.

Step 3

Deselect any parts of the report that you would like to omit, then click "Save". Your report will open in a new tab.

How do I give teachers access to the admin panel?

The best way to give teachers access to the admin panel is to create an account for them.

Step 1

Go to the admin home page. Click "Users".

Step 2

Click "Add Report" in the top right corner.

Step 3

Deselect any parts of the report that you would like to omit, then click "Save". Your report will open in a new tab.
